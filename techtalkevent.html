Tech Talk

As future civil engineers, are we expected to limit ourselves in learning only the technical aspects of the course? Have we thought about the ways to hit the peak of success in a construction project? We are to be well adept in overseeing every aspect regarding a project including planning, execution, monitoring, control and closure. Project management enables us to come out as experts on latest construction technologies with the perfect execution methods and integration.

PANTHEON 7.0 presents TECH TALK to enlighten us on "Construction Management and Associated Risks" to help us widen our aspects on better execution of our knowledge and skills.  To give us insights on the topic, we have B. Harikrishnan, Project Director, Habitat Group PMC Division.
