Focus Photography Competition

To capture a moment with all its elements synchronizing harmoniously is beyond the mere click of a button, it's a skill that many crave. PANTHEON 7.0 presents "SYMMETRY", a photography contest to bring out the best shutterbug amongst you. Claim your prizes by registering at the link given below Rules and regulations:

  • The Contest is open only to college students.
  • The entry should contain the participant’s Name, College, Department, Contact number and Instagram username.
  • Entries are to be sent to the given link in JPEG format.
  • The entries should be sent to the above-mentioned link before the deadline.
  • A maximum of 1 entry is allowed per person.
  • Minor digital adjustments( cropping, colour enhancement ) are allowed in order to optimize an image. Any alterations to the original photograph, not listed here are not allowed and will render the entry ineligible.
  • Images should not have any watermarks or captions on them.
  • Copied entries, if found with valid proofs, will be disqualified.
  • Images will be shortlisted based on the number of likes and comments on Instagram. Shortlisted images will be further judged by the jury.
  • The decision of the jury panel shall remain final and binding.
  • JUDGING CRITERIA
    Relevance of the Theme, Composition, Technical accuracy, Creativity, No. of likes and comments on Instagram.
