About Us

ICI CET chapter along with ICI Trivandrum chapter brings you the 7th edition of Pantheon on 29 and 30th of April. Since 2013, it has acted as a platform to share ideas, thoughts and challenge the existing norms of construction by developing innovative proposals. Activities including tech talks, workshops, inter collegiate competitions are conducted to enhance the skills of students and to provide them with insights into the professional world that help them to choose their career paths.

Pantheon 7.0 provides you with a platform to unleash your true potential, creativity, technical skills and be handsomely rewarded for it. We welcome all the passionate minds out there to trigger the change and spark the revolution. Success awaits the ones who strive for it.
